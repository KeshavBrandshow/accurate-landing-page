About Accurate Group

Committed to Academic Excellence & Holistic Development Since 2006

Established in 2006 under the Accurate Education & Research Society, Accurate Group of Institutions has grown into a leading higher‑education group in Greater Noida and Delhi NCR. It offers programs in Management, Engineering, Pharmacy, Architecture, Law, and Diploma studies, with a strong focus on academic excellence, holistic development, and industry‑oriented learning that empowers future leaders.
