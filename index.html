D.Pharm

Diploma of Pharmacy

D.Pharm. equips pharmacists with fundamental knowledge of formulation, standardization, rational use, and safe dispensing of medicines, including exposure to both modern and traditional systems, as well as prescription drugs. This broad training creates multiple career opportunities for diploma holders and helps meet the rising demand for qualified pharmacists in an evolving healthcare system.

  • Strong pathway to start a career in pharmacy.
  • Trains in rational use and proper dispensing of medicines.
  • Covers modern, traditional, and prescription medications.
  • Opens multiple career opportunities for D.Pharm holders.
  • Offered by Accurate College of Pharmacy, a reputed institute.
